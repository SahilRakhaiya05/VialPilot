What it does

VialPilot runs a full observe → reason → act → verify → replan loop for laboratory automation. Upload a bench image or MP4 video, describe your task, and watch specialist agents plan safe robot moves in a browser-based 3D lab.

Vision→ Decompose→ Localize→ Safety→ Plan→ Act→ Verify→ Report
